Project Proposal

My website will be about fitness information, including different workouts and supplements I find to be helpful

The objective of my website will be to provide useful information to people who may not have access or want to add to their current knowledge

My target audience will be people beginning in the gym or people who have been going for a long time and want more information

My page will have a section for workout information, a section for supplement information, and within each of those will have different article type pages to provide a in depth summary of what each of these things does
